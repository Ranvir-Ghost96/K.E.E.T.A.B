A suggestive learning application for IITU students

"Welcome to our online library! KEETAB (Knowledge Everywhere EveryTime And Beyond) Our digital platform offers around-the-clock access to a vast and diverse collection of digital resources, including e-books, and previous year question papers. You'll benefit from user-friendly search features, real-time content updates, and interactive tools that enhance your research and learning experience. By going digital, we reduce costs, preserve resources, and ensure global accessibility. Our secure and scalable system also provides valuable data insights to continually improve our services. Explore, learn, and collaborate with ease through our online library."
