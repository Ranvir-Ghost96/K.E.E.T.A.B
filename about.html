Our Mission

The primary goal of our efforts is to digitize and efficiently organize our extensive library resources. This endeavor aims to enhance accessibility and provide an improved user experience for all our patrons. We are committed to offering a diverse range of digital resources, ensuring that our collection remains up-to-date and continually expanding. Our objective is to enrich the online library repositories with an array of features that will further enhance the overall user experience.

Our Vision

An online website and suggestive learning application for IIITU students, where students can access the books and PYQs. The Online Library Management System project aims to overcome the problems that are faced in manual library, processes with a digital platform that streamlines operations, improves resource management, and enhances accessibility. The system will automate tasks, provide 24/7 access to resources, manage user accounts, and ultimately revolutionize the library experience for both staff and users.
